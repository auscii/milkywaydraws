FAQ

How do I commission a piece of art?

To commission , please visit the "Commission" page on our website. There, you'll find detailed information about the process, pricing, and an order form to submit your request.

What types of artwork do you offer?

I specialize in anime and chibi artworks. You can explore my portfolio and commission to see examples of my work.

How long does it take to complete a commission?

The time to complete a commission varies depending on the complexity and my current workload. Generally, it can take anywhere from one week to several weeks.

What payment methods do you accept?

Gcash and Paypal.

What if I need to cancel a commission?

If you need to cancel your commission, please contact me as soon as possible via email and we can discuss.

How will I receive my completed artwork?

Digital artworks along with the other variations will be sent to you via email in high-resolution format

What if I have more questions or special requests?

Feel free to contact me directly through my email address. I am happy to answer any additional questions and accommodate special requests whenever possible.
